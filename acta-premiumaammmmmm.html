How it works
A clear process.
Attended meetings.
We handle everything before the meeting. You handle everything in it. Every step is designed to make sure the conversation that lands in your calendar is worth having.
01

You brief us — we become you

We learn your agency, your offer, your pricing, your tone. Every call we make is in your name. Prospects experience your agency from the first conversation.

02

We build your prospect list

Targeted research into local businesses that match exactly what your agency wants as a client. Every call is deliberate. We do not dial randomly.

03

We make the calls

Daily outbound calling, handling objections, pushing through to the decision-maker. Most opportunities are lost after two attempts. We keep going.

04

Our standard

No show means no charge

If a business does not attend the meeting we booked, we do not invoice. We rebook at zero cost. Your money leaves your account only when the meeting took place.

About Acta

We do the
prospecting your
agency keeps
putting off.

"The best agency in the room wins nothing if nobody brings them a business to work with."

Acta was built around one observation: marketing agencies are among the most capable businesses at growing other companies — and consistently among the worst at filling their own pipeline. It is not a skill problem. It is a bandwidth and appetite problem.

Cold outreach is hard, repetitive, and demoralising. It pulls your team away from the client work you are actually paid to do. Acta exists to be the prospecting arm your agency does not have. We handle everything before the meeting — the research, the calls, the objections, the qualification. You handle everything in it.

We focus on prospecting local businesses for agencies. Trades businesses, construction companies, hospitality venues, retailers — the kinds of clients that need marketing, that have budget to spend, and where a direct phone call to the right person changes the outcome. We work on a pay-per-attended-meeting basis. You pay when results happen.

01

Results only

We do not invoice for activity, calls made, or strategy sessions. You pay when a decision-maker attends the meeting we booked. That accountability keeps us sharp.

02

We represent you

Every call is your call. We study your agency, your pitch, your positioning. Your prospects experience you from the very first conversation — before you have spoken to them yourself.

03

Local business focus

We specialise in prospecting local businesses for agencies. The kinds of clients in your own city where relationships move fast and the right conversation at the right time wins the work.
